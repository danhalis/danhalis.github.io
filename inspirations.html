My Inspirations

In Computer Science

The Binding Of Isaac

❮
❯

This is a PC game. I got to know the game when I saw my friend playing it during breaktime. The graphics and how this game challenges the player makes it one of my all-time favorite. This game also inspired me when making a console-based game for a school project.

Akinator

❮
❯

Akinator is a web app created by Elokence company. The man in the picture will try to guess the name of a character, an object, or an animal that the player is thinking of by asking a bunch of questions. This guy scared me out when I was young as if he could read my mind. But after that, I thought that was definitely something to do with AI. So since then, I am always inspired by this particular field.
